Hello,

I’m The66Monkey — the kind of person who pokes at Linux until it either behaves or explodes in a way I can learn from.
This blog is my running log of experiments, hacks, half‑finished ideas, and the occasional “fine, I’ll rewrite the whole thing” moment.

I founded [Scandinavian Hacking](https://scandinavianhacking.se/), as an attempt to fund the hobby and share my experience and knowledge with others.

LLM's trims the research time, but the real work happens at 2am with a mug of coffee and the stubborn belief that the code will bend before I do.
Curiosity starts the project. Spite finishes it.

If you want to see the wreckage and the wins, check out my [GitHub projects](https://github.com/The66Monkey)
